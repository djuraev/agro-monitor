What is index insurance?

Index insurance is innovative tool for crop producers, where the insurance pay-outs made based on “index” that correlated with crop losses.

 

Advantages:

  • The payout is based on an objective index that the farmers or insurers cannot influence
  • Low cost due to reduced administrative costs
  • Timeliness of payouts as there is no need for assessing individual grower in-field area losses

 

In particular, as an index the average regional yield losses based on the vegetation condition measured by satellite is considered

 

Insurance contract is fully based on satellite measures that removes all possible influences by human
